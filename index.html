Hey, I’m Brian.

I’ve been the “scattered one” my whole life — bouncing between ideas, jobs, and creative projects. ADHD, mental health struggles, and self-doubt were part of my daily reality.

But I learned how to turn that chaos into something powerful. I host the Scattered podcast, share stories about mental health and geek culture, and help people who feel like they’re “too all over the place” discover that they’re not broken — just wired differently.

Scattered Nation is my way of building a community and a toolkit for people like us — a place to get clarity, build confidence, and create something real.
